Our Platform

Our platform is built using the latest app and web technologies available to make your experience as personalized as possible and to fit your weekly needs.

Nutritional Recipes

The meal plans are produced based on Little Lunches’ expansive collection of breakfast, lunch, dinner and snack recipes. The recipes have been developed by our team of highly trained chefs and dietitians and tested by kids (of course!)

Social Responsibility

For every subscriber on Little Lunches, lunch meals are provided to school children in need in the Americas. We are working with various charity organizations to serve public schools in multiple cities and rural areas.
